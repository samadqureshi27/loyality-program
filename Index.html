FAQ

Questions? We've got answers

General Questions

Do my customers need to download a new app to use the loyalty card?

+

No! Our digital loyalty cards live directly in the Apple Wallet or Google Pay apps that are already pre-installed on almost every smartphone. This removes the friction of downloading a new app and ensures your customers are always ready to earn rewards.

How do customers add the card to their phones?

+

It's as simple as scanning a QR code at your checkout counter, clicking a link in an SMS, or via your social media profile. Once they scan, the card is added to their digital wallet in two taps.

Is there any specialized hardware required?

+

Not at all. You can use our free Scanner Web App on any existing smartphone, tablet, or laptop to scan customer cards and issue rewards. No expensive proprietary hardware is needed.

Features & Automation

How does the Geolocation Push Notification work?

+

When a customer is within 330 feet (100 meters) of your business, their phone will automatically receive a lock-screen notification. This acts as a powerful "digital sign" to remind them to stop in while they are nearby.

Can I send messages to my customers for free?

+

Yes! Unlike SMS, which carries a per-message cost, our platform allows for unlimited, free Push Notifications. You can send updates about new promotions, holiday hours, or personalized offers directly to their lock screens.

What happens if a customer doesn't visit for a long time?

+

We have an automated "Reminder" feature. If a customer hasn't made a purchase within a set timeframe (e.g., 28 days), the system will automatically send them a personalized notification to encourage them to return.

Business & Integration

Can I customize the look of my loyalty card?

+

Absolutely. You can fully customize the colors, logo, images, and text to match your brand identity. You can also choose between various card types, including Stamps, Cashback, Membership, or Tiered Discount cards.

How do I track the success of my loyalty program?

+

You will have access to a comprehensive Analytics Dashboard. Here, you can track your total number of customers, retention rates, average check size, and even see who your "Top 10" most loyal customers are.

Does the system support multiple locations?

+

Yes. Our platform is built to scale. You can manage multiple branches from a single dashboard and see which locations are performing best.

Privacy & Security

Is my customer data safe?

+

We take data privacy seriously. All customer information is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with standard data protection regulations to ensure that both your business and your customers are protected.

What if a customer switches to a new phone?

+

Because the cards are synced with Apple and Google accounts, their loyalty card and earned points will automatically transfer to their new device when they sign in.
